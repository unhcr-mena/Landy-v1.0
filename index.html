One platform to coordinate.

assessment, referral & assistance

RAIS is a web-based assistance management platform used by UNHCR, partners, and donors to ensure effective tracking of assistance and enhanced accountability.

RAIS was first developed in 2009 by UNHCR Jordan to address the demands for a more coordinated approach for the delivery of refugee assistance.
